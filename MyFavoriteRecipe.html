Summer pancakes!

These are not the normal swedish pancakes that I grew up with. Instead they are American pancakes with a hint of lime, usually served with maple sirup, exquisite! I chose this recipe because it reminds me of my visit to my friends in California.

Ingredients

Topping

  • 1 tbsp Icing sugar / Powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp Maple syrup

Pancakes

  • 1 tbsp Granulated sugar
  • 1 Eggs
  • 2.5 dl milk
  • ½ Lime
  • 2.5 dl Wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Baking powder

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine all the dry ingredients.
  2. Pour in the milk, egg, melted butter, and lime juice.
  3. Mix until the batter is smooth and well combined.
  4. Fry pancakes about 10 cm (4 inches) in diameter in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Serve with maple syrup and a dusting of powdered sugar.
